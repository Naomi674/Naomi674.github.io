Why ICT?

The HBO_ICT program suits me because I am a detailed oriented person who also has the patience required to take the time and solve the problems presented to me. Another reason I am well suited for the ICT program is because I love learning and the ICT field is constantly growing and changing which requires the people in the field to be constantly willing to learn to stay on top of the changes. Lastly I am well suited for ICT because, I love being faced with problems/challenges and then working to better understand why the problem occurred and how to fix them.
